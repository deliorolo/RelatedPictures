How to play

▶ This game was created to be played by two teams (blue and green) of minimum two elements each (minimum four players).

▶ An element from each team (two people) are able to unveil the colors that belong to each image without the knowledge of other players.

▶ From the total of twenty pictures, there are eight for one team, seven for the other, four neutral (yellow) and one game killer (red).

▶ The team with more initial pictures always starts the game.

▶ The element aware of the colors is going to tell a single word that can be related with the biggest number of their pictures.

▶ The other elements from the team are going to guess a picture through the given word. In case the selected picture is a match, they can keep the turn and select the next one.

▶ In case the team selects a picture from the other team or a neutral, the turn changes and the same procedure is repeated by the other team.

▶ Everytime the turn changes, a new word is given. When one team finds all the pictures that belong to them, the game is over and this team is the winner.

▶ If any team, any time, selects the red picture the game is immediately over and the opponents are the winners.


Have Fun!
